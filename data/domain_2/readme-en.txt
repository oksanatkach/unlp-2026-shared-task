This folder contains instructions for the medical use of medicinal products. All instructions are written in Ukrainian. The average length of an instruction is 8 pages.

Each instruction includes the name of the medicinal product, which is often provided in two forms: the official name in Ukrainian and the international name.

After the name of the medicinal product, the main sections of the instruction follow:
- composition, indicating the active substance and excipients;
- pharmaceutical form;
- pharmacotherapeutic group;
- pharmacological properties, describing the mechanism of action of the product, its pharmacokinetics, and pharmacodynamics;
- indications, listing the diseases and conditions for which the product is used;
- contraindications, listing the conditions and factors under which the use of the product is prohibited;
- interactions with other medicinal products;
- special precautions, providing recommendations for use in specific patient groups or in the presence of comorbidities;
- method of administration and dosage, recommended for patients of different ages;
- overdose;
- adverse reactions/effects, grouped by organ systems and sometimes indicating the frequency of adverse reactions;
- shelf life;
- storage conditions;
- packaging, specifying the quantity and form of the product in one package;
- legal classification â€” prescription-only or over-the-counter.

At the end of the instruction, there may be information about the manufacturer, its location and contact details, as well as information about the marketing authorization holder.

The instructions may contain data presented in tables.

Instructions for the medical use of medicinal products are an important source of information for healthcare professionals, pharmacists, and patients, providing the necessary information for the safe and effective use of medicines.
